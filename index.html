English Level Assessment Test

A1 to A2 Transition - Comprehension & Writing Skills

📋 Test Instructions:

This test assesses your English comprehension and writing skills at A1-A2 level.

Duration: Approximately 45-60 minutes

You will encounter multiple-choice questions, fill-in-the-blanks, reading comprehension, and writing tasks.

Section 3: Reading Comprehension A1-A2

📖 Read the following passage and answer the questions:

My Daily Routine

My name is Maria. I am 22 years old and I am a student. Every day, I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning. I have breakfast at 7:30. My breakfast usually consists of bread, butter, and tea. After breakfast, I go to university at 8:00. My classes are from 9:00 to 1:00 PM.

After university, I go home and have lunch at 2:00 PM. My lunch is usually rice, vegetables, and chicken. In the afternoon, I study or do my homework. At 6:00 PM, I have dinner with my family. We like to talk about our day during dinner.

In the evening, I relax and watch TV or read books. I like to read books about adventure and mystery. I go to bed at 11:00 PM. I usually sleep well at night.
Question 11
How old is Maria?
Question 12
What time does Maria have breakfast?
Question 13
What does Maria usually have for breakfast?
Question 14
When does Maria go to bed?
Question 15
What kind of books does Maria like to read?

Section 5: Simple Essay Writing A2

Essay Question 1
Write a short essay (100-150 words) about your favorite hobby or activity.

Guidelines:
- What is your favorite hobby/activity?
- Why do you like it?
- How often do you do it?
- Who do you do it with?
Essay Question 2
Write a simple description (80-120 words) of your daily morning routine.

Include:
- What time do you wake up?
- What do you do first?
- What do you eat for breakfast?
- How do you go to school/work?
Essay Question 3
Write a short thank you letter or email (100-150 words) to someone you appreciate.

Include:
- A polite greeting
- Reasons why you appreciate them
- A specific example
- A proper closing
